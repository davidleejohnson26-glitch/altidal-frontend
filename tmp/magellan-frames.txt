Empty Leg
Private Flights
A Unique Travel Opportunity

Empty Legs occur when a private jet needs to be repositioned to another destination, and is flown without passengers on board. Thus provides travelers with a unique opportunity to see what it's like to fly as a Magellan Jets Private Client at a reduced cost.

Experience the luxury of private jet travel at a fraction of the cost with Magellan Jets’ Empty Leg Flights.  These one-way charter flights offer significant savings while maintaining the high standards of a full-price private jet charter. Ideal for spontaneous and savvy travelers, empty leg flights blend flexibility, comfort, and affordability seamlessly. Booking an empty leg flight lets you enjoy private jet travel at a much lower price, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a premium travel experience without the premium price tag. Empty leg flights often become available at short notice, making them perfect for those with flexible schedules. If your travel plans align with an available flight, you can take advantage of this last-minute option for convenient, private travel.  Whether you need a light jet for a short trip or a larger aircraft for international travel, Magellan’s empty leg flights cater to a variety of travel needs on top-tier aircraft such as the Citation X, Hawker, and Gulfstream G IV, each offering luxury, comfort, and premier amenities.
